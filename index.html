Frequently Asked Questions

Is BetControl Free To Use?

Yes! BetControl’s core features, including Budget
Tracking and the Report Card, are completely free.

Will BetControl stop me from placing bets?

Yes, BetControl blocks access to betting sites or apps during self-exclusion periods or when limits are reached, helping you stay in control.

How does BetControl help me manage my gambling?

BetControl helps you manage your gambling by offering tools to set budgets, track spending, and gain insights.

Does BetControl work with all betting platforms?

Yes! You can manually log your bets, no matter
where you place them.

How does the Budget Calculator work?

It helps you set limits on your spending and track
them, so you stay in control.

Is my data safe with BetControl?

Yes! We use encryption and never share your data
with third parties.
